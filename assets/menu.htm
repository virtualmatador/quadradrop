How to Play
  1. Press the "Play" button to open the game board.
  2. The game starts at paused state. Press resume button to play.
  3. Use one of these methods to move and rotate the falling piece and complete horizontal lines.
    • Swipe to move, tap to rotate, long press to drop.
    • Use the on-screen controls.
    • Use left, right, and down arrow keys to move, up arrow key to rotate, and space bar to drop.
  4. Clearing several lines at once awards more points.
  5. Tap the back button at the top-right corner to return to this page.
End of Game
  • The game ends when a new piece cannot enter the board.
  • Every ten cleared lines increases the level and falling speed.
  • To play again, press the "Reset" button.
  • Resetting the game clears your score and line count.
