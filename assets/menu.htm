How to Play
  1. Press the "Play" button to open the game board.
  2. The board opens paused so you can get ready.
  3. Press Pause to begin, then move with the arrow keys or on-screen controls.
  4. Move and rotate each falling piece to complete horizontal lines.
  5. Clearing several lines at once awards more points.
  6. Swipe or use the controls to move; tap the board to rotate.
  7. Use Drop or the Space bar to place a piece immediately.
  8. Tap the back button at the top-right corner to return to the menu.
End of Game
  • The game ends when a new piece cannot enter the board.
  • Every ten cleared lines increases the level and falling speed.
  • To play again, press the "Reset" button.
  • Resetting the game clears your score and line count.
